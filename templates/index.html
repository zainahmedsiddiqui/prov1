The population of Pakistan represents 2.56 percent of the world´s total population which arguably means that one person in every 39 people on the planet is a resident of Pakistan. Pakistan's latest estimated population is 212,742,631 According to the 2017 Census of Pakistan. This makes Pakistan the world's fifth-most-populous country. It is estimated that Pakistan will be the 3rd largest Population country by the year 2050.

The motto of the country is Unity, Discipline, and Faith. The name Pakistan means Land of the Pure in Persian and Urdu. It is officially called the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.
